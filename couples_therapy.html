Counselling and therapy for couples

Home > Therapy > Counselling and therapy for couples

Bernadetta Janusz and Anssi Peräkylä provide together (in co-therapy) family and couple counseling and therapy.
The aim of the therapy and the counseling is to improve relationship satisfaction and conflict resolution between couple and family members. We focus on understanding recurrent problematic patterns in relationships as well as major developmental challenges including loss of a close one. In our work we are inspired by psychoanalytic and systemic paradigms.
We work with families and couples facing challenges related to individual personalities, those navigating grief, and those representing diverse cultural backgrounds.
Counseling and therapy can be in-person or online, or in a combination of both. The sessions are conducted in English.
