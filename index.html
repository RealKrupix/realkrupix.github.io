About us

Bernadetta Janusz and Anssi Peräkylä are psychotherapists, researchers and university professors. They offer individual and couple therapy along with supervision and workshops. In their work they combine clinical insights and research to understand connections between people.
Particularly, they focus on re-connecting: establishing connections again after crisis, loss, and life transitions.

Psychotherapy and supervision

Our work enhances processes of re-connection in emotional and relational problems, after loss, in complicated or prolonged grief, an in major life changes.
