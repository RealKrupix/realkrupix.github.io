Our mission

Bernadetta Janusz and Anssi Peräkylä are psychtherapists, researchers and university professors who offer individual and couple therapy, supervision and workshops. The key idea of their work is combining clinical insights and research to understand connections between people.
Prof. Anssi Peräkylä, psychoanalyst and professor of sociology, brings a profound understanding of interaction and self to his psychotherapy practice. His passion is conversation analytical research: study of language and interaction that illuminates in a new way the connections between persons in the therapeutic processes.
Prof. Bernadetta Janusz, a clinical psychologist and anthropologist, has more than two decades of experience as couple and individual therapist, supervisor and trainer. Her expertise in mentalization, loss and grief shapes her therapeutic work. She understands therapy as a process of reconnecting with those whom we live, and also with those whom we have lost.
In addition to their clinical work, Anssi and Bernadetta also provide research-based supervision, enhancing clinicians’ skills and understanding of the interactional processes that connect clinicians and their patients.
