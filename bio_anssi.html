Prof. Anssi Peräkylä

E-mail: anssi.perakyla@helsinki.fi

Anssi Peräkylä is a psychoanalyst and professor of sociology at the University of Helsinki. He received his PhD from the University of London in 1992 and became a member of the Finnish Psychoanalytic Society and the International Psychoanalytic Association in 2013. In his clinical work, he specializes in questions of self and identity. As a psychoanalyst, he represents a relational and interaction-centered view. Peräkylä has published his works in leading international journals in psychoanalysis, psychotherapy research, and sociology. His books include AIDS Counselling (Cambridge University Press, 1992), Conversation Analysis and Psychotherapy (co-edited, Cambridge University Press, 2008), Emotion in Interaction (co-edited, Oxford University Press, 2012), and New Perspectives on Goffman in Language and Interaction (co-edited, Routledge, 2023).
