Group supervision: loss and grief

The group is for therapists working with Complicated Grief and Prolonged Grief Disorder. The group is also suitable for clinicians working with anticipated loss (e.g. terminal illness) or ambiguous loss (e.g. related to neurological diseases). Currently group supervision is conducted in Polish (in the future, also possible in English).
