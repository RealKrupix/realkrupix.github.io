Research-based group supervision

Bernadetta Janusz and Anssi Per채kyl채 are leading an international research supervision group. The participants receive basic training in conversation analysis: in producing video-recordings, anonymising and subtitling them, in producing detailed transcriptions, and in analyzing interactions sequentially. During the academic year, each participant produces videos and transcripts of their own therapeutic practice. These data are analysed together in the group. The videos and the analyses made of them serve as the basis for supervisory discussions on the participants' ways of working with their patients. The group meetings are online; a six hour meeting takes place once a month.
