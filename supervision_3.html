Supervision-seminar group

Home > Therapy > Supervision-seminar group

Supervision-seminar group will meet once a month (4 hours), including 2 x 15-minute breaks. The group will be based on the psychoanalytic, systemic and attachment approach. In the seminar part, we will read the latest texts on the process of individual, couples and family therapy. Supervision-seminar group conducted in Polish (possible in English at a later date).
